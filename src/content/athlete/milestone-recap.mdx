There’s a phase of training where nothing feels smooth. Your body is adapting, your timing is off, and every session asks for more.\n\nThis is the part I’ve learned to respect. If I can keep showing up with intensity *and* composure, I get better—and the people around me feel that stability.\n\nMy rule: **be hard on the standard, kind to the process**.
