I love being competitive. I love training until the session is honest. But what keeps me in dragon boat is how **collective** it is.\n\nWhen the boat moves, it’s because everyone is aligned. That changes what “success” means: it’s not just output—it’s trust, rhythm, and shared belief.\n\nMy goal is simple: **push my physical limits while uplifting the people around me**. If I’m getting faster but the crew isn’t, I’m missing the point.
